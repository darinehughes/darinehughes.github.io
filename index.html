What I do

Hughes Virtual is my independent studio for applied work in conversational AI, simulation, and extended reality. I build systems where people can practice real conversations, receive targeted feedback, and connect those results back into training programs.

Focus areas

  • AI driven avatars for teacher, student, and customer conversations
  • Rubric aligned scoring and conversation analytics
  • Integration with simulation, XR, and medical training environments
  • System design and technical writing for research and proposals

Current work

I am a co founder and technical lead at AICS, LLC, a company focused on conversational AI for practice and coaching.

Through Hughes Virtual I also support projects that connect conversational tools to larger training ecosystems, including XR medical and point of injury simulation systems.

About

I hold a PhD in Modeling and Simulation. My background spans XR medical trainers, point of injury systems, serious games, accessibility oriented virtual environments, and conversational coaching platforms for education and defense partners.
