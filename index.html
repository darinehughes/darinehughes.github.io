What We Do

Hughes Virtual LLC is a research and development company focused on turning advanced simulation, artificial intelligence, and educational technology concepts into practical systems. We support universities, government partners, healthcare programs, and commercial teams developing tools for training, coaching, reflection, and performance improvement.
